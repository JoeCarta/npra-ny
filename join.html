Become a Member
Join a Coalition of Change‑Makers
NPRA-NY is a nonpartisan alliance open to community leaders, professionals, grassroots organizers, and mission-aligned organizations across New York State.
Member Benefits
  • Access to exclusive networking events and chapter meetings
  • La Agenda-NY newsletter delivered to your inbox
  • Voting rights in organizational decisions and elections
  • Leadership development and advocacy training opportunities
  • Connect with Puerto Rican leaders and organizations nationwide
  • Access to the Member Bulletin Board: legal, realty, and scholarship resources
  • Support advocacy initiatives that move the needle for our community
Membership Types
Individual Member
Community leaders, advocates, professionals, and any individual committed to NPRA-NY's mission.
Affiliate Organization
Nonprofits, community organizations, and businesses aligned with our mission who wish to collaborate and be listed in our network.
